What Are Dental Crowns and Why Are They Important?

A dental crown is a cap placed over a damaged or weakened tooth to restore its size, shape, strength, and appearance. Crowns are a popular restorative solution for teeth that are cracked, decayed, or heavily filled. At Dental Crowns, we provide a range of materials for crowns, including porcelain dental crowns, ceramic dental crowns and zirconia crowns, to suit different needs.
